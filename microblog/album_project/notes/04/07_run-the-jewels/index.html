  • AHHH mike’s verse that starts 36” chain is INCREDIBLE- best verse on here so far imo :))

  • the HUGE beat of the next track DDFH right after is so damn heavy and incessantly catchy :O

    • this has the best beat imo, so mf tasty ^

    • the mf dolphin lmaooo

  • I like a lot of the elements of no come down, that chorus especially; while repetitive, it feels so fitting for a track with this aesthetic
    • not my favorite, but not a bad cut at all ^
  • holy moly, get it has yet again another incredible beat behind hypnotizing bars; killer mike’s second verse especially is absolutely amazing :O

  • that tourette’s line is completely stupid and I love it :))

  • those huge, stretched-out bass and kicks for christmas fucking miracle are jaw-dropping yet again… I love this thing so damn much
    • the last few lines from el-p especially are a standout for me; killer mike’s ending and inspirational referencing of ice cube is yet another great line to complete this whole shocking experience of an LP
