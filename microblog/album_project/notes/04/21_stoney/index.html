mini notes for stoney

  • okay honestly, that weirdly-styled set of guitar riffs are honestly not bad, I’m just not really in with the lyrics as much; that beat isn’t bad too, so for a debut album’s first track, not bad ^^
  • big lie is one of those inoffensive cuts; that hook is honestly not bad at all, I’m just not blown away at all
  • I get no feeling from deja vu though, it’s like scrolling through instagram, feeling cathartic wasting your time there- the justin bieber is really what seals the deal for me :c
  • okay I have to be real though, no option is a fantastic little banger in the midst of this snore fest… that beat and bass descent is just such an ear-candy moment :)
    • not an incredibly interesting track, but everything just comes together right for me; it’s playful and entertaining with its production value and especially post’s floaty vocals
    • ROLEX WITH THE DIAMONDS
  • good jeff verse?
    • lol anyways, cold has a really interesting, icy beat at first… but everything else about the track is just meh; post’s lyrics aren’t interesting, and the snaps/claps on the beat are just incredibly boring :(
  • middle school track incoming; I won’t mince words, but this track, while iconic, just isn’t super compelling anymore? it’s just something that’d be in the background while you have a much more interesting conversation with your friends about better music
    • the bass is the most interesting part of this track, and while the come-up story of post is not something I’ll ever bash… this is not the track that represents his best work
  • fall apart is kind of a sad boy track, but in an inoffensive way—it’s perfectly okay in that lane, and I won’t criticize it for that
  • patient, on the other hand… I just do not like this thing at all, it’s just a boring auto-crooner cut in the midst of an okay string of tracks :c
  • go flex is a more interesting cut, with that sung hook from post going over pretty nicely; good stuff :)
  • feel just feels (haha) meaningless and a bit boring, not really contributing much of anything, and reveling in some weird sexual motifs
    • like yea, it’s fine, but this is another soul-crushing instagram/tiktok track :(
  • too young is another sleepy cut, there’s not really anything to say about it, it’s just begging to be skipped
  • god damn, the more I listen to congratulations the more I hate it; it’s the other super iconic track off of this album, and for a bit of a good reason, but I just can’t stand it anymore
    • it comes together in a rickety, basic, and incoherent way at times :/
  • big surprise, a track with pharrell behind it isn’t all that bad lol
    • up there is another pretty good moment honestly, it’s respectable in its theme and the self-actualizing way about it :)
  • yours truly austin post isn’t a bad track, and it ends pretty well; nothing could’ve been better I guess
