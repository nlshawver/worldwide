mini notes for bon iver, bon iver

  • don't know how much I'll like this style... but this first track is at least surprisingly interesting and compelling in how it's articulating itself :o
    • that was an incredible transition, kinda shocked at that and how well it went :)
  • with those crunchy synth bass lines under the verses, minnesota, wi is a great little number to come right after ^
  • don't know entirely how I feel about this LP yet, but holocene is a really beautiful kind of track; lovely vocal motifs and I rly like vernon's voice at times like these :)
  • and while I like the general change in style of towers, it’s not a huge standout in terms of replayability or anything for me
  • michicant has a really pretty start to it though, the vocals are on point ^
    • the small, quick change of scenery in the instrumental is pretty cool too ^^
  • ooh wow, the deep voice kinda got to me on hinnom, tx :o
    • was very brief and to the point, but in a very nice way; it felt perfectly intoned for this project :)
  • damn that piano on wash. is beautiful :O
    • that was incredible; might be my favorite individual cut on the record, even if I think that first three track run was really damn good too ^
  • calgary was a rly nice follow-up though, good stuff- and with lisbon, oh acting as a nice lil interlude, this is carrying itself well to be a great conclusion :)
  • yea beth/rest is another beautiful track that caps off this album nicely; it’s not my favorite with some of the vocal timbre and almost organ-like instrumental… but I’m definitely not complaining, cause it was just really nice overall :))
