mini notes for swimming

  • honestly, just kind of a somber, sort of sleepy, yet appealing start; not bad for an album fantano gave a three lol
  • hurt feelings is just a bit of a nice, slightly punchier resolution into the themes of this record; it’s a pretty good track I guess :)
  • the thundercat bass has blessed the third track :D
    • while I initially thought the lyrics were just a bit tugging at artificial heartstrings, it turned around pretty quickly- and the chorus / late verses are lovely too (even if I’m still not the biggest fan of mac’s voice all the time :)
    • that mainly instrumental bridge is real nice too ^
  • perfecto just kinda left a bit of an average chill track taste in my mouth, until that nice little ending; it curved it around in an interesting way, I like that ^^
  • self care has jid, blood orange, and erykah badu? woah :O (just learned they’re not really ~on~ the track, but still cool)
    • that beat is insane under mac’s voice; it fits better than I would’ve ever thought ngl :)
    • the chorus is the best part so far ngl ^
    • absolutely lovely beat flip into the second part :)
    • the theme and meaning of this track is generally just freezing- it kinda just leaves you wondering, pondering the mental conflicts and pressures of what mac was going through; it’s haunting and hopeful ^
  • above all else, it earns that runtime, and the transition into wings was as good as it could’ve possibly been :)
    • and honestly, while wings was mostly just kinda there for most of its own runtime, that final minute and a half was actually rly good imo ^
  • both small worlds and conversation pt. 1 are surprisingly good to me; I don’t thinks its standout necessarily for this record, but it’s still great :)
  • dunno is another track that takes me a bit to warm up to, but I really love its aesthetic and the way it flows :))
  • wow... I don't know whether its the ethereal voice on the chorus or the careful confidence of the verses that makes jet fuel a genuine standout cut for me, but I don't rly care :D
    • the lyrics definitely help :)
    • the ending is wonderful too, such a trip ^^
  • and then 2009 and the final track are a stellar end- I can't say the ending is as standout as I'd have hoped, but it's still lovely- great ending to a surprisingly good album :)
