mini notes for what's the 411?

  • I was a bit confused and bored w the opener, but I do like the thematic choice of it- and then the beat of reminisce came in and just exploded with such an appealing, unique style :D
    • genuinely, I absolutely love that track ^^
  • YOOOO frank sampled this cut :O
    • I'm loving this record a lot rn; real love has a punchier beat but all the same niceties and confident swagger that reminisce has :)
  • you remind me is a great follow-up... I'm really searching for faults rn, because this is literally everything I could've asked for so far :O
    • that lil wind instrument at the end is a great little addition :)
  • haha busta rhymes's part is so playful and ridiculous; nice bit of interlude ^
  • god her voice is even more ethereal (or rather, sort of angelic) on sweet thing; still the same amazing aesthetic :)
  • love no limit was the first time I realized a lot of the album is maybe a bit overly similar or consistent- still a good track, and i don't want to do anything is another fantastic cut right after :)
  • okay yea, slow down was pretty good, but I'm starting to get that aching feeling from hearing the same, similar sort of beat and lyrics for fifty minutes- again, it's not a dislike at all, it's just getting a bit worn now
  • my love is a bit better, but it's still the same feeling unfortunately :/
  • changes i've been going through is a bit better tho- the beat is punchy, and everything feels exactly in the right place ^^
  • and the final title track is the closest thing to a return to how I felt before- it has a bit more punch to it, but in the end it just sort of sizzles out for me...
