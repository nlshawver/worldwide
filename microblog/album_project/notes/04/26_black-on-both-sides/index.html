  • yea it felt weird going straight back to pretty much a black star track right after (even if I like it a lot)

  • damn, and the meaning, feature of q-tip, and general stature of… track fifteen… is absolutely lovely, and one of those additional perspectives that keeps this LP interesting (and my mind attuned properly to that which other people experience)
