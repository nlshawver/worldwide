An Evening With Silk Sonic by Silk Sonic

Silk Sonic Intro

you can tell exactly how good this is gonna be just by this intro, damn…

silk… /sonic/

Leave The Door Open

honestly some of the lyrics are cheesy in the first verse, but the instrumental and vocals outweigh any of that

it might be a bit overblown for my taste at the crescendo, but the chorus ties it back down well (especially that nice dissonance at the end)

yea, in the final chorus I’m not a huge fan of Mar’s straining in his voice, but you just can’t deny how smooth his voice sounds when he immediately goes more to a more mellow intonation right after

Fly As Me

ooh, that’s nasty already, what the f- we’re four seconds in and that’s nasty? that’s some betty davis shit

seriously, this is so unbelievably good; Mars and Paak together are so likeable, it’s probably my favorite overall track out right now, such a banger

AND THAT BASS AT THE THIRD VERSE WITH PAAK

UH
SOMEBODY THIS FLY (OWWWW)

perfect track, no hats

After Last Night (with Thundercat & Bootsy Collins)

I don’t know how you transition away from that high point, but honestly that Collins intro to both Mars & Paak singing simultaneously is a really good way

that first chorus is actually really good, a bit unexpected but yet again Mars has some wack vocal line he pulls out of no where that’s stunning

this is such a horny album lmao

I do think that last bridge is probably the best part of the track in its lead up to the end chorus, it compliments in a very nice way

damn this duo is already legendary

Smokin Out The Window

wait a minute

and now this is such a catchy, appealing– lustful yet hateful song, this is the clear popular star off this record, and I get why
