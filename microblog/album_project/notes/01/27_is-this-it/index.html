notes for january 27

Is This It by The Strokes

Is This It

I don’t think this is particularly transcendent or incredibly detailed, but that’s not what it’s trying to be; the guitar and bass lines are perfectly simplistic, and for the start of an indie rock record, this serves its purpose really well imo :)

The Modern Age

was a bit unsure about this track at first, with the marching drum line not doing much for me, but that bridge and of course the chorus was actually pretty good

Oh, in the sun, sun, having fun, it's in my blood
I just can't help it
Don't want you here right now, let me go

and damn that guitar solo is wonderful :O

I do like how it feels like a veil is lifted off the instrumental when the chorus starts up, that’s a bit of a banger :)

Soma

that guitar riff, while repetitive on its own actually fits surprisingly well in this - I do like the aesthetic of this track and the record so far

it may just be recency bias or something similar, but none of these tracks seem incredible yet :/

but damn that outro was stunning :O

Barely Legal

oooh that opening guitar riff was damn good ^^

okay this is the standout track so far to me, that layered and in-sync instrumental and vocal line is executed so well :D

and damn, that third verse is iconic

Oh, I didn't take no shortcuts
I spent the money that I saved up
Oh, mama running out of luck
And like my sister, don't give a fuck

okay this energy just suits this so much better; it took only a little bit but this aesthetic so far is really good

Someday

this track doesn’t stick out to me as much, but the simplistic primary guitar line and drum beat do pair really nicely together

and as usual the chorus is quite well done, and has a good amount of meaning behind it with the theme of the track ^^

okay nvm this was a pretty good interlude if you think of it like that, it’s nice :)

Alone, Together

ooh I like those leading guitar chords on the main verses, and damn that transition to the chorus is surprisingly good :O

okay my god that outro is absolutely crushing; that’s fantastically done

Last Nite

yea we all know this one

everything kind of comes together for this track, these last four tracks have been a really good peak for the album so far :)

Hard to Explain

honestly the part that sticks out the most to me is that first chorus, that was stellar even if I was wary for the first half of the track ^

yea my god, that pre-chorus to chorus is such a great moment, this is a great continuation of the energy

When It Started

okay I forgot I had the U.S. version- either way though, this is a great sort of fast paced track to split from the last song

I think that second verse and chorus is the best, most expressive and interesting part of this track, I liked that :)

Trying Your Luck

I do kinda like this guitar riff- and the most exciting part of this track for me is that second verse and chorus yet again

I’m honestly not sure about this record anymore- it peaks really well, but it feels dragged out already even if it’s less than forty minutes

each of these tracks would be wonderful on their own, but as a cohesive unit I think this thing suffers for me the most

good ending tho

Take It or Leave It

this does seem like a good end, but I’m just already checked out

I’m sorry, this album was worth it, but the sum of its parts together feels much weaker than each track on its own

unfortunate ;(
