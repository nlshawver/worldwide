notes for january 13

self-titled (blue) by Weezer

My Name Is Jonas

okay we all already know this

this song is such a classic, and that second verse… OOH

My name is Wepeel, got a box full of your toys
They're fresh out of batteries, but they're still making noise

god everything just fits so snugly, it’s all so well-done; the entire instrumental at the outro is amazingly bombastic, with a soothing acoustic ending

No One Else

this song is absolutely ridiculous, and it fits exactly the type of energy you see on the album cover :)

it’s like exactly what a thirteen year old thinks about romance; it’s so pleasingly outrageous and bombastic, it’s not even a popular song of the album and it’s such a damn catchy and well-made satirical ballad

The World Has Turned and Left Me Here

god and I love the meaning behind the last two songs: this guy was just being so blatantly sexist and awful, and this song is just about the girl breaking up with him because of it, and him wondering why lol

I talked for hours to your wallet photograph
And you just listened
You laughed, enchanted by my intellect
Or maybe you didn't

this is the best verse on this record so far, i love this ^

and my god what an earworm, everything on this record is so beautifully done you just can’t forget any of the energy shown

another amazing outro, right into:

Buddy Holly

WEEE OOOOH

oh my god this song- that synth/string whatever right before the second verse is absolutely amazing on such an amazing track

OH OH

this might be absolutely the best Weezer song to scream your heart at to; it’s just absolute alt rock perfection, everything meshes and molds right together into this masterpiece

and you cannot tell me that guitar break is not absolute FIRE

Undone (The Sweater Song)

and we’re right into another classic, and I think the perfect downtempo transition from Buddy Holly

If you want to destroy my sweater
Hold this thread as I walk away
Watch me unravel, I'll soon be naked
Lying on the floor, lying on the floor, I've come undone

this chorus is absolutely stunning, I can’t stop just loving every second of this thing

I’ll stop bringing them, but seriously these instrumentals are so damn good; and that final quiet, unsettling outro is such a nice, weird break :O

Surf Wax America

this album is classic after classic, my god

that’s such a minimalist, marching chorus that just meshes so well with Rivers’s vocals and the furious guitars alongside him

and MY GOD that acapella chorus with a bit of an aggressive undertone right at the end- right into this AMAZING instrumental is fantastic

Say It Ain’t So

but then you get this absolute masterpiece, so…

god, I don’t think I need to say anything about this track, everyone knows everything about what makes this such a stunning master-class of alt rock

with how smooth and silky the rest of the track is, and that small guitar solo after the first chorus, all the choruses just feel absolutely crushing :O

AHHHHH THE GUITAR IN THE CHORUS

from the second chorus to the end of the song is quite possibly the best two minutes of music I have ever heard

and my lord the meaning of this song is absolutely heartbreaking, five out of five

In the Garage

I don’t know how you can come down from the last track, but seriously this might be the best way- it’s like an escape from the pain of the last track

the repetition of the verses fits exactly right with the tone of the track; I like that a lot, and my god the bass on the third verse is so strong under the vocals

and a fantastic little guitar solo yet again ^

Holiday

god I love how this just feels like a victory lap (and it pretty much is, since this and In the Garage were made right after their signing of a record deal)

and I really, really like how simplistic and emotive that short little chorus is :)

(On the road with Kerouac)
We will write a postcard to our
(Sheltered in his bivouac)
Friends and family in free verse
(On this road, we'll never die)

I absolutely love this bridge; and my god if the rest of this song just feels so stunningly excited- and AH that second-long guitar break :D

Only in Dreams

this is the perfect track to cap off a perfect record in my eyes; the slowly introduced instrumental is and always will be a classic, and the lyrical style and intonation is so smooth :)

Only in dreams, we see what it means
Reach out our hands, hold onto hers
But when we wake, it's all been erased
And so it seems, only in dreams

this is a lovely chorus ^

and honestly that second verse is even more stunning, it feels so soulful and it just slowly builds right back into that marching chorus; this outro may be long but all the instrumentals and incredible, and the build-up is so damn amazing

yea this absolutely does feel like the perfect end for a nerd album: he doesn’t get the girl :(
