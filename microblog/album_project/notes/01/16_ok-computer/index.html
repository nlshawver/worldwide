notes for january 16

OK Computer by Radiohead

Airbag

ooh this introduction is really good, I'm liking this so far with very typical vocal styling from Thom

and I really like how warped and alien this instrumental turns into, with the driving guitar following right after it

Paranoid Android

god and I love this track so far, with the acoustic guitar section over these more tame vocal sections- but right into this incredibly aggressive bridge

the structure of this song is both mind numbing and completely sensible, I love that

and this second bridge is so unsettling, and in such a good way

god this second instrumental break is crushing, that's incredible :O

Subterranean Homesick Alien

The breath of the morning I keep forgetting
The smell of the warm summer air
I live in a town where you can't smell a thing
You watch your feet for cracks in the pavement

that is a really somber start, that sets the mood really damn well

and those rhodes keys are a really great fit :)

jesus that transition back into the second chorus got me good, that's amazing ^

Exit Music (For a Film)

ooh wow, these vocals hit so well; I love it

and with the backing lines that develop behind him, damn ^^

jesus, that's such a crunchy bass line, that's incredible with Thom's vocals :O

Let Down

that was a really good transition, I'm loving this record so far :)

the guitar riff that persists throughout this track is really nice, it's a great continual line through it

god and that beat that sort of just appears out of nowhere with a little over a minute left got to me ^

this felt both hopeful and absolutely crushing at the same time, I love it

Karma Police

oh my god, that buzzing bass with the keys throughout the whole track- I don't even know how to describe this song, it's just haunting and lackadaisical simultaneously

jesus christ what an outro :O

I just don't know what to think, and I think that's the point

Fitter Happier

this is so unsettling, jesus christ

the theme of this record is getting to me, it's so hyper-focused on modernity and life as a concept

Electioneering

but damn what a fantastic sound on the following track, it feels so foreign after last track :O

I think this is the highlight of this record so far just because of that, it's such a driving, furious track and I adore it :O

Riot shields, voodoo economics, your turn, your turn
It's just business, cattle prods and the I.M.F
I trust I can rely on your vote

and that verse- my god

Climbing Up the Walls

jesus this album is such a trip, everything is just so threatening and terrifying without even being outright aggressive for much if it

this track exemplifies that to a tea

No Surprises

what the hell

why is there a glockenspiel :O

okay wait this is actually really good

my god that first verse is genuinely shocking, I adore that sort of contrast given the tone of the track

and that is a lovely outro, probably my second favorite track (or at least second most notable track) in all honesty :)

Lucky

my lord, this track is an absolute treat as well :O

all the vocals and instruments fit so well, it's a lovely combination and tone

yet again another stunning outro too ^

The Tourist

yet again another soaring, somber piece of work- I love it

Sometimes I get overcharged
That's when you see sparks
They ask me where the hell I'm going
At a thousand feet per second

god that's so good, and I love the fit of the overblown guitars right after the following chorus :)

fantastic end to a stunning record ^
