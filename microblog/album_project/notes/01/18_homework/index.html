notes for january 18

Homework by Daft Punk

Daftendirekt

ooh that’s a funky cadence :)

I’m not really sure what I’m going to think of this, given I know the least about this era of Daft Punk

I was starting to get worried about this track, and when the drums and that screeching cymbal come in it kinda peaks- but this is just the opening track, and that groove is really nice

WDPK 83.7 FM

nice transition ^^

haha I love that introduction, that’s pretty nice

Revolution 909

that’s a nice little interlude before the beat, which is way more shrill than I was expecting- but the bass kinda carries this imo

the little downturn into the beat again was nice though, and this is a good beat for sure, I’m just not completely sold

ooh and that blatant stutter in the beat at the very end is rly nice, but my point still stands that it just feels a little bland

Da Funk

but ooh this introduction is stellar already, what the hell

OHHH SHIT this beat :O

and my god that reintroduction of all the parts of the beat at around the halfway mark of the track is wonderful :))

that was really damn good, I just hope the rest of the tracklist supports that clear high point with that driving sample

Phœnix

I’m not gonna lie this isn’t doing it for me so far

yea that bass line that comes in at the middle was the most surprising thing on this track

I think this track does kinda grow on me though, it just might be too lightweight of a track to truly be special to my ears

Fresh

ooh okay, this is a better start compared to the last track, I’m liking this steady, warbling overtone with this flowing and receding beat

and that’s a nice fade-out; I find the instrumentation on that much more appealing than on Phœnix, while being much more brief

Around the World

okay okay, this is another funky start, good stuff :)

hahaha I love the wikipedia page for this song:

The song's only lyrics are "around the world," said 144 times.

I don’t think this has nearly the prestige and overall lovely aesthetic of Da Funk, but this is certainly a jam ^

Rollin’ & Scratchin’

okay, I don’t know if it’s just me but I can only mesh with some tracks on this record; this is not one of them

like I appreciate the instrumentation and the wild, screeching synths, but it just doesn’t constitute much of the praise that I’d give the other two/three tracks that I think have more merit

this might be the most adventurous track so far though, so I give it credit there, since it’s at least interesting- I just think it might overstate its welcome in spite of that

okay that outro was nice though, I liked that given the track’s context

Teachers

ooh wow okay, this is an interesting, weird sort of track

I think it might have stayed a bit too long, but it was a worthwhile break

High Fidelity

I do like the increased amount of samples on this track as opposed to their very tame use on the opening half of this record- but I think it more so shows the rest of this record as being less adventurous than it could have been

the second half of this track is I think the peak of it, and it’s a genuinely really good deep cut on this album

Rock’n Roll

yea, not a huge fan of the beat in this context yet, but those grossly distorted synths punching in and out are a nice addition; this is definitely not a high point to me though

that drum beat introduction is good tho, this is getting interesting

it’s just completely ridiculous, I don’t know- it’s not halfway through and I’m already sick of it

it just for too long walks the line between incredibly driving and catchy, and mind-numbingly frustrating- so it’s just gross after so long :(

Oh Yeah

that’s actually a catchy, punchy, interesting interlude, so a nice break from the previous track ^

Burnin’

okay this is actually a pretty good start, but I think those zipper-esque noises are just weird- and it just keeps going

like when they go away the beat sounds so transcendent and beautiful, but then they come back and just want to tear my ears off

LIKE AHH the beat is so damn good, but that noise is just disgusting- and that little bass melody they throw in is so good

this song genuinely makes me angry

Indo Silver Club

and another good start, I really like this weird cadence to the groove, it’s done really well

unlike Burnin’ that weird synth guitar actually works really well, and isn’t just absolutely grating; it adds to the aesthetic so well imo ^

OHHH shit yea this song absolutely slaps- lovely ending too :)

Alive

creepy lil intro; bass and drum intro sounding really spacious and well made

that was honestly really good, might be one of the top tracks on this album because of how well it mixes together the instrumentation ^

Funk Ad

ooh, weird little backwards interlude :O

okay, that’s all
