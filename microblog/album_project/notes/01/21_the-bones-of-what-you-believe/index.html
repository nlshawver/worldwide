notes for january 21

The Bones of What You Believe by CHVRCHΞS

The Mother We Share

ooh okay, I was really worried for this track at the onset, but the actual beat and vocals relieved me a lot

I do really like this aesthetic, I think it has a lot of personality and merit behind it especially given those soaring synth lines ^^

god I love that ending chorus, that’s absolutely stellar :O

We Sink

I thought the instrumental was a little derivative at first, but god the crunchiness of that bass is amazing

and jesus that chorus was surprisingly good, and the shimmering instrumentation is a really neat thematic choice

I think it does get a bit repetitive but it’s rly well made imo

Gun

god this start is a groove and a half

AHHHHH THAT PRE-CHORUS TO THE CHORUS

I don’t know how or why I had such a jaw-dropping reaction to that but jesus christ that’s such a playfully toned, incredibly punchy part of this track

absolutely fantastic track, and the choice of having it fade out is always a nice choice imo

Tether

and that’s a really nice and much more mellow introduction, I like the very pulsating synth lines :)

my lord that interlude is absolutely mesmerizing, I’m adoring this ^^

another extremely solid track, this is a very strong first four

Lies

god that’s such a unique sounding instrumental behind this track, I’m adoring the energy of this record so much

and ooh that last chorus is so wonderfully punchy; I have very little to say just because I feel at a loss for words at how unique, crushing, and absolutely lovely these synths are :)

Under the Tide

okay okay, I thought this was gonna be kind of a sleepy track, but the second chorus convinced me that this is just an overall good track, it’s just not nearly as standout as the opening- five songs

those two high, melodic synths really add to this though, I think it just comes together well on the backend

so I guess it just got better as I went along- especially with those incredibly warped drums :O

Recover

I think honestly the verses of this thing shine the most, some of the instrumentation isn’t getting to me, but there’s enough to it that I do like it a good amount

I feel like this is the actual slight downturn even if the chorus and post-chorus is done really well- it just feels like camouflage in the midst of this record

well okay I say that but these backend recoveries and emotional leaps are really getting to me ^^

nvm wth how does this record not dip at all yet?

Night Sky

okay I really liked the verse, but my GOD that chorus is so damn good :D

this is probably one of my top three tracks so far, and I love the little 808 noise? I don’t really know what it is, but it’s like a plucky drum

jesus this song is absolutely stunning with the soaring vocals :)

and that ending is scampering along, I like that thematic choice given the rest of the track

Science/Visions

and that’s a rly good transition into this RLY nice arpeggio :O

absolutely lovely bass line, I like that a lot ^^

I’m going to be honest, that chorus might be my favorite on the whole record so far, that’s so lovely :)

I guess it helps that the instrumental is an absolute mf BANGER too my god

Lungs

this is yet again another track I was initially put off by, but the chorus sounds as close to introspective & mellow as this thing gets

and that absolutely crushing, crunchy bass, I adore that ^

you can bite it, wth :O

By The Throat

yep this is the theme of this record- slight unease about the thematic choices, like the stereotypical electronic clapping leading to the chorus

but DUDE MY GOD that sparkling aesthetic of the chorus is such a trip, this might be my favorite track :D

such a wonderful energy to the track, and it’s not grossly slick or anything close to it; yea this is the definite favorite, such a soaring anthem :)

You Caught the Light

I do really appreciate how this record ends, actually giving a sort of conclusion to the mayhem of the previous eleven tracks- especially given the last definitely felt like the album’s peak

absolutely lovely album from start to finish, I’m so glad I included this thing :)
