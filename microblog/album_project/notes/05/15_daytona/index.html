mini notes for daytona

  • alright this ye production is already doing wonders for this project, my god; that first bridge and the second verse of if you know you know is incredible :D
  • damn I love that goya montoya line on the third verse of the games we play; I definitely like the more plain lyrical ability on this track, even if the instrumental does more wonders on the opening cut ^
  • okay okay I really like hard piano, especially the way the instrumental shifts just slightly in line with rick ross’s verse; I have a bit of waning confidence though, but I’m holding out ofc
  • that george jackson sample is amazing, and a really great contrast / connection, serving as the chorus; the verses are pretty good too, but I’m still a bit skittish… not sure why, but am
  • that refrain from 070 shake is really damn good, and I do really like the way the beat lingers in a subtle way at push’s third verse; that’s probably the closest track that’s been as interesting to me (right now) as the opener ^
  • what would meek do is a pretty good cut too, and I like the bounces between ye and push; that maga hat line was iconic though wtf :o
  • infrared is a standout closing track though, with all those digs against birdman, drake, wayne… the messaging over a relatively subtle instrumental and great sample is a good closer to this project ^
