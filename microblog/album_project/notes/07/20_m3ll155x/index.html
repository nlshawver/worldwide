mini notes for m3ll155x

  • I don’t even know what to think with figure 8; that harsh, explosive synth instrumental that shears into the track after her first verse is incredibly intense, and the following content continues in a really interesting, hard-to-describe way :o
    • just kinda at a loss for words for what this collectively is…
  • damn, that grating patch in the final pre-chorus, that stems into that crescendo of the last chorus on i’m your doll is incredibly captivating
    • it’s such a weird, manic style that I think is both incredibly disjointed, but communicates these intense feelings really well; it’s a first listen, so I can’t possibly digest all that’s underlying here about past and present interpretations of love- but it has easily some of the most striking visuals (both lyrically and instrumentally) that I’ve heard in a little while :O
  • god that chorus is incredible, w those repeated exclamations of “you’ve got a goddamn nerve” :ooo
    • this dense mf instrumental is so beautiful, I can’t get over how much it adds to this EP :D
    • AND THE BEAT DROPPING OUT AND SWITCHING :O
    • easily the most excitable track for me so far, and that slow fade to nothing is a great closer for the midpoint here ^
  • holy shit… no words for how almost haunting that hook on glass & patron is :ooo
    • this whole track is a confusing web of articulate explosions, and I’m just in awe at how it comes together ^^
  • I don’t even have a thing to say about mothercreep; I’ve looked into a bit of what it’s getting at, and I’m just awestruck rn
    • was not expecting this level of density in an EP, and I’m just stepping back and thinking…
