mini notes for in utero

  • serve the servants takes a really good starter position on this record, with the chaos that ensues almost immediately serving as a fantastic re-ascension for a final LP :)
  • jesus christ the screams on scentless apprentice are absolutely wretched, I can’t not appreciate the grating cacophony :O
  • heart-shaped box I think is the only radio hit from this record (besides maybe it’s successor on the tracklist, toned down), and it absolutely deserves the recognition
    • especially after its predecessor, it’s a surprising and eccentric comeback from what is essentially hell on the last track
  • honestly I was a bit let down from what I remember rape me to be, and how I was expecting it to wring from the subtle yet aggressive sound… but it’s obviously not bad at all, and frances farmer will have her revenge on seattle right after is a banger follow-up :D
  • also, did not realize I knew dumb- this is another great cut here :)
    • that cello is an absolutely wonderful addition to this track, I love it so much more with that added complexity :D
  • very ape is a great bordering-on-interlude into the off-putting disorder of milk it; it’s such a creeping, immense experience with cobain’s vocals sauntering under everything… until that chorus :O
    • that CHUCKLE :DDD
  • pennyroyal tea is of course also a great hit to this record… this LP somehow lives up completely to its predecessor :O
  • radio friendly unit shifter and tourette’s are, as the latter name suggests, are chaotic messes—but they’re absolutely beautiful :)
  • all apologies is such a beautiful closer for this record
    • it may not have the broad strokes of the other hits here, but the simple melody and sustained bass turn this into an incredibly poignant send-off
