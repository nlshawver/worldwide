mini notes for malibu

  • the bird is such a great introduction to this record and paak's style, and heart don't stand a chance just follows so well right in its footsteps :)
  • it's hard to describe the slick sort of style and skill anderson paak has, but jesus if the waters doesn't show it off perfectly :O
  • and somehow it keeps getting better with the easy feel of the season / carry me... LOVE that k.dot line (and the flow that follows) :D
  • have to compliment the bass throughout this whole record so far, it's a bit under and away from the mesh, but it's ever-present and feels absolutely lovely to my ears~
  • and oh my god what a transition in between put me thru and am i wrong
    • outstanding feature from schoolboy q too :O
  • the rapsody feature has a great, powerful sort of articulation that contrasts paak's styling in a rly nice way- but god damn parking lot is a classic :)
  • honestly light weight is the only sort of underselling cut here so far in the first half hour- but only in the fact that it’s a bit minimal and almost like a long interlude
  • room in here feels just coated in braggadocious swagger, I love it :)
    • I don’t know what it is about it, but the game somehow fits really damn well in here :D
  • actual interlude moment; haha I love when the vocals come in on your prime, so jokingly real and genuinely funny :)
  • what the hell is the start of silicon valley HAHAHA
    • that knocked it down by half a star all by itself lmao
    • I have no idea how this cut can sound so mf impressive with such outlandish, ridiculous lyrics :o
  • the dreamer is absolutely wonderful, it’s a great sort of romp with a legend that all just ends on a high note :)
  • it’s a great but weird set of ending tracks, I love it <3
