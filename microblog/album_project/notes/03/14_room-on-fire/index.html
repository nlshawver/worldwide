mini notes for room on fire

  • despite the positive review of their debut record is this it, I think I still was a bit too harsh on it for calling it par for the course in terms of indie/alt rock—just the first moments of what ever happened? are captivating in its own unique aesthetic, and I really like it :)
  • and I feel like it’s absolutely necessary to point out that for such a record to only be thirty-two minutes is a shocking amount of restraint; this thing could easily be much longer, but it never wastes a moment of your time ^
  • oh shit I know reptilia, this is a banger; best track of the two so far :D
  • automatic stop is a great emotive follow-up, with that tinge of despair coming through really well at the end ^^
  • jesus this record is exactly what I needed rn- 12:51 isn't my favorite here, but it's still a great cut and its ending is as great as the others present; you talk way too much is a fantastic little ballad in the middle of this absolute romp :)
  • while it's not as close to it's preceding track's simplicity, between love and hate has a really endearing swagger that bookends the cut nicely ^^
  • that's what she said
    • shit i dropped my ipod
    • but that was a good track
  • under control is somehow really unique and interesting despite the extremely similar styles- I feel like I might love this LP
  • I don't know what to say about the way it is, it's just good ^^
  • and both ending tracks are wonderful, what an incredible finishing duo :)
