mini notes for section.80

  • oh my god... I don't even know what to say for these first three tracks; A.D.H.D. caps the start off well, but all of these cuts have a kind of careful consideration and very deliberate feel to them :D
    • and my god if the instrumentals and samples aren't wonderful ^^
  • the double entendres are just so easy coming from kendrick's vocals- but jesus christ that second verse on no makeup is absolutely crushing... :o
  • chapter six is a great, subtle shift over to ronald reagan era... but that cut is just so insanely detailed, I love it ^
  • the storytelling throughout poe man dreams is equally as impressive imo (and the "my simplest shit be more pivotal" line is just... perfect)
    • glc has a great few parts on this cut too, his presence is a nice second voice in this piece
  • while the spiteful chant is definitely not my favorite cut, I have to be honest in saying the beat is insane and a lot of the verses are great as usual ^
    • holy shit how is schoolboy q so good on other people's tracks :D
    • that combo verse just turned around this track, I love this now :)
  • I have no words for keisha's song... it just has such immense power behind it
  • it is slightly weird it goes right into rigamortus, but I'm not complaining because rigamortus is such an insane, lyrically-dense romp of swagger :D
    • THE BASS :OOO
  • kush and corinthians honestly surprised me a lot, I adore that third verse and into bj's verse- kendrick just feels so slick during the verse and comes to in a very maddening inflection during the chorus :O
  • both blow my high and ab-soul's outro are lovingly crafted, but each have their own strengths I think as ending pieces- and my god if jazz doesn't fit so perfectly with kendrick's cadence and emotion :D
    • thank u terrace martin for the sax ^^
  • and hiipower caps this thing off so mf well... the repeating lines from verse to verse and kendrick delivery together makes this so much of a fantastical finishing track :)
