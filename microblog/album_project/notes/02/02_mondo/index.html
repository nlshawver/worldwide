notes for february 2

Mondo by Electric Guest

Holes

ooh that’s a bit of a weird introduction, but I cannot yet tell whether it’s interesting or just chaotic for the sake of it

honestly the lyrics are pretty compelling, and this serves as a good introduction I think— not bad at all :)

This Head I Hold

especially when the next song is this ^^

it is a bit of a muzak, lobby sort of song, but it is pretty catchy and compelling as just a simplistic melody :)

I do think the chorus is almost symbolic, and doesn’t really represent anything significant, but the verses at least have some personality on their bones

Under the Gun

the same cannot be said about this track though, at least right now; the introduction just feels like scrap, and it just feels like filler :/

that chorus actually has potential I think, the layered vocals sound smooth and silky… but when it goes back to these verses, I just feel off-put

this outro feels gross too, with the marching drums and the background synths not really being interesting at all

Awake

wait okay this isn’t bad at all- I really like those layered vocals and the way they travel around the melody; I feel like this will be a theme, where sometimes the tracks hit pretty well, and the rest just fall flat for me

yea, and that outro was pretty mellow, and had an interesting aesthetic (even if I’m not a huge fan of those synths at the end)

it’s a very passable indie pop track, but I hate to say that that’s probably the peak of this record :c

Amber

okay that chorus is pretty good with those Danger Mouse synths ^^

I just feel like the verses are kinda milquetoast; while I like the double entendre between amber being a person and the tree amber, it just feels uninteresting by the second verse

that key change was a good ending, but I can’t help but feel like it was not earned at all :c

this definitely isn’t a bad track, it becomes more interesting after its midpoint, but it’s just not all that interesting

The Bait

ohhh I know this one too- honestly this isn’t bad either, it just feels incredibly sterile

and that’d be an interesting style if it was both intentional and the music or theme was compelling in that style… it’s just not very

Waves

yea and this one’s even worse for me; it’s so grossly over-done, the vocals don’t really match, and this fake sort of melody is just tiring :/

I’ve been pretty checked out of listening for at least ten minutes now

and that perfectly describes the aesthetic and (lack of) energy on this record for me

Troubleman

okay exactly- this first half of this long track is actually kinda nice, and probably the most intriguing portion of this backend-

but it just should have ended at the halfway point… because of this second part I just completely forget everything of how we got here, and the story that’s attempted here just doesn’t land for me :c

the fact that I’m waiting for this track to be done shows how compelling it is; there are plenty of artists that can make an eight minute track worthwhile, interesting, and overall a great experience that you remember

none of this sticks with me, and that is exactly the problem

American Daydream

nope to all of this

that first verse with the weird, dissonant layered vocals, and the plucky synth under it just don’t hit with me

and it continues throughout the whole track

oh my GOD even the lead singer (who is the brother of the guy FROM THE LONELY ISLAND) sounds disinterested- why do you expect me to be happy about listening to this when you don’t even want to be here, wth

Control

yea I’ll stop, you get the vibe of my thoughts
