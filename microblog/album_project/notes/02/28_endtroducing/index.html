  • the bass sample from orion was what got me really appreciating the sampling work on this record :O

  • I just can’t help but think that so much of this record is tied with its reputation; it certainly influenced a great many musicians, but it’s just not connecting with me to that shining, flawless level that so many other publications and reviewers think of it
