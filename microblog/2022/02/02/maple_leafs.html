maple leafs

I have discovered exactly what I failed to realize monday–and only just realized today because I set a reminder about it over a year and a half ago. It it with heavy heart that I must say:

It has officially been 20,000 days since the Toronto Maple Leafs have won the Stanley Cup.

That is all that I have to share today. Take it in. The second of April, 1967—the date the trophy was awarded, and the Canadiens were beaten 4-2 in the series final—was exactly 20,000 days ago.

It's impressive, really.
