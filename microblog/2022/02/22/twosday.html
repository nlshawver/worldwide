twosday

Well... here it is. The most important and incredibly interesting holiday ever.

It is kind of surreal that something like today's date is so rare, and somehow actually kind of funny to think about. I don't really think there's much to it, obviously, and it's way more anticlimactic than I could've ever expected. But it's still charming in its novelty :)

Hopefully the one and only twosday you and I experience is a good one, and that life treats you well. For the sake of the 'holiday,' take a break; you can relax a little, and maybe do something two themed. Here's some ideas:

  • watch a movie that has only one sequel, like
    • the Kill Bill series
    • both (good) Ace Ventura movies
    • Tron and Tron Legacy
    • both Mamma Mia movies
    • and both Paul Blart: Mall Cop movies
  • listen to a double LP, like
    • The Wall – Pink Floyd
    • Physical Graffiti – Led Zeppelin
    • Speakerboxxx/The Love Below – OutKast
    • Bitches Brew – Miles Davis
    • Sign o' the Times – Prince
  • watch a two-part Youtube series, such as
    • The Bob Emergency by Secret Base
    • The Big Money Space Race by Internet Comment Etiquette with Erik
    • How to be a Pirate by C.G.P. Grey
    • The End by Eddsworld
  • or only watch the second Hobbit & Lord of the Rings movies :)

~ hopefully that satisfies your requisite two-ness for the rest of the day ~
